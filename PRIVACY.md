# Privacy Policy for Audio Transcription Extension

        This Privacy Policy describes how your personal information is handled in the Audio Transcription extension.

        **1. Data Collection and Usage**
        We do not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information. The extension's core functionality relies on processing audio locally.

        **2. Audio Processing**
        All audio captured from your browser tab is sent exclusively to a local server running on your own computer (localhost). The audio data NEVER leaves your machine to be processed by our servers or any third-party cloud transcription service.

        **3. Third-Party Services (Google Translate & Gemini API)**
        To provide real-time translation, the transcribed text (and only the text, not the audio) is sent to Google's translation services (Google Translate or the Gemini API). If you provide a Gemini API key, its usage is subject to Google's own privacy policy and terms of service. We do not have access to your API key or the content you translate.

        **4. Data Storage**
        The extension uses local browser storage (`chrome.storage.local`) to save your settings, such as preferred language, UI preferences, and your Gemini API key. This data is stored only on your computer.

        **5. Contact Us**
        If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
